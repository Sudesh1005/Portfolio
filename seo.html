Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is all about improving a website’s visibility in search engine results. I implement SEO best practices to ensure your website is easily discoverable by users searching for your services or content online.

From optimizing meta tags, headings, and URLs to ensuring fast load times and mobile responsiveness, I build websites with SEO in mind right from the start. I also focus on clean, semantic HTML and structured content to enhance search engine crawling.

Whether it’s a static website, a React SPA, or a dynamic Next.js app, I apply both on-page and technical SEO strategies to boost performance and ranking. Better SEO means more traffic, more engagement, and better results for your business.
